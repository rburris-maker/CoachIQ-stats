FAQ

Questions

Do I need any special equipment to use the live tracker?+
No. Just your phone. Open the app in your browser during the game and tap to log stats. It works on any smartphone — no app download required.
How are player ratings calculated?+
Ratings are calculated using a position-weighted formula. A goalkeeper is rated differently from a striker — the system accounts for the stats that actually matter for each role. Goals, assists, passing accuracy, defensive actions, and clean sheets all factor in based on position.
Can I use this for multiple teams — Varsity and JV?+
Yes. You can create separate teams for Varsity, JV, and JVB. Each has its own roster, games, game plans, and stats. You switch between them with one tap. The tryout module even lets you move players directly to the right roster when you close tryouts.
Is my data safe? What happens if I cancel?+
All data is stored securely in the cloud. Your data is private — only you can see it. If you cancel, your data remains accessible on the free plan. We don't delete anything.
Can players see their own stats?+
Yes. You can set a PIN per player and share a link to their profile page. Players enter their PIN and see their season stats, rating trend, and recent game history — no login required. You can also email individual match reports after each game.
Does it work on a phone during a game?+
Yes — the live tracker is specifically designed for one-handed sideline use. Large tap targets, grouped stat categories, auto-timer, and a player grid so you never have to search for a name mid-match.
